Fiesta Tableware Company Fiesta Colors Guide

Touted as 'The Most Famous Dinnerware in the World', Fiesta has been a fan-favorite since its introduction in 1936. Designed in the bold Art Deco styling by Frederick Hurten Rhead, Fiesta dinnerware encourages you to mix and match for the perfect tablescape.

Unofficial color guide for Fiesta Colors from the beginning in 1936 to today, includes original and post 86 colors. This project is not affiliated with or endorsed by The Fiesta Tableware Company.
